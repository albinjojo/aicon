About the Conclave

A flagship dialogue on AI, not another lecture series.

AI Conclave 2026 is a two-day flagship event bringing artificial intelligence out of the lab and into three sectors that shape everyday life in Kerala: Agriculture, Health and Education. Rather than one-way talks, the conclave is built around dialogue — a panel discussion that puts practitioners and academics in conversation, hands-on department-led workshops, and a hackathon where students build.

Organised by the AI Club and Student Council of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, in association with the Departments of Computer Applications, Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Electrical & Electronics Engineering.

2 Days
2000+ Expected participants
3 Sectors — Agri, Health, Edu
5 Departments co-organising

Format: dialogue-driven, not one-way talks — panel discussion, parallel workshops, and a hackathon in place of a straight lecture track.
