About the Conclave

A flagship dialogue on AI, not another lecture series.

AI Conclave 2026 is a two-day flagship event bringing together practitioners, researchers, policymakers and students to examine how Artificial Intelligence is transforming three sectors central to Kerala's economy and society: Agriculture, Health and Education. The Conclave is built around real dialogue — sessions are designed for participants who can describe genuine ground-level problems, share evidence from practice and stay engaged for follow-up discussions, rather than one-way presentations.

Participants stand to gain real, sector-specific value from attending. Farmers, FPOs and agri-tech innovators get direct access to AI tools for precision farming and crop advisory, alongside dialogue with KAU, KVK and ICAR scientists. Doctors, health-tech start-ups and public health officials can explore AI-assisted diagnostics and rural healthcare delivery models. Teachers, students and education policymakers gain exposure to AI-enabled learning platforms and direct interaction with industry mentors. Across all three sectors, the Conclave offers a channel to influence policy through dialogue with government officials and invited dignitaries.

Organised by the AI Club and Student Council of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, in association with the Departments of Computer Applications, Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Electrical & Electronics Engineering.

2 Days
2000+ Expected participants
3 Sectors — Agri, Health, Edu
5 Departments co-organising

Format: dialogue-driven, not one-way talks — panel discussion, parallel workshops, and a hackathon in place of a straight lecture track.
